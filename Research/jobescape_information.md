# JobEscape — Comprehensive Research Brief

## What Is It?

**JobEscape (jobescape.me / app.jobescape.me)** is a mobile-first AI education + freelance career platform. The core promise: teach non-technical people to build AI agents and automation workflows, then monetize those skills as freelancers. Zero coding required, everything step-by-step. It sits at the intersection of edtech and the gig economy, sold as a career-change vehicle.

## Business Model

**Subscription SaaS + mobile app funnel**, with a quiz/chatbot front door that hides pricing until after emotional investment.

### Entry Funnels
- `jobescape.me` → quiz assesses "where are you now" → personalized plan → price reveal
- `jobescape.me/chat-v3` → chatbot asks about experience level → pushes urgency ("Last chance to master AI")

### Subscription Tiers

### Money-Back Guarantee
Heavily marketed but nearly unclaimable. Requirements: complete ALL courses + follow the plan for 28 consecutive days in the first 31 days + fail to land a client within 3 months + submit proof within 31 days after that window. Refunds excluded for partially used periods; EU gets 14-day cooling-off.

## Positioning & Strategy

**Core tagline:** "Build & Sell AI Agents — No Experience Needed"

**Emotional hook:** FOMO + economic anxiety ("Don't get left behind. Last chance to Master AI")

**Target audience:** Non-technical career changers and side-income seekers who feel left behind by the AI wave — ex-drivers, office workers, business owners wanting to add AI skills.

**How they differentiate:**
- Mobile-first (vs. desktop-heavy Udemy/Coursera)
- AI tools bundled into subscription (not just courses)
- Hyper-specific niche: AI automation *for* freelancing
- Strong guarantee messaging to overcome price objection

**Stated competitors:** Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer — misleading; those are marketplaces. Real competitive set is edtech: Coursera, Udemy, Skillshare, and niche AI course platforms. Jobescape *trains people to work on* freelance platforms, not compete with them.

**Geographic reach:** US, Canada, Australia, Europe — English-first global expansion.

## Controversies & Weaknesses

1. **Hidden billing:** Users report unexpected $88 charges not clearly disclosed upfront; some banks flagged payments as potentially fraudulent
2. **AI-generated content:** Multiple critics say courses are AI-written, not taught by experienced practitioners
3. **Tool bundling criticism:** All "included" AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) are publicly free or cheap separately
4. **Founder opacity:** Miras Sovetov is the only named person; instructors are anonymous
5. **Refund trap:** The guarantee is real on paper but virtually unclaimable in practice given the requirements
6. **Overpromised outcomes:** Implies rapid freelance income; skips the hard parts (portfolio building, client trust, real-world practice)

## Inside the App: Authenticated Product Structure

*Based on direct product knowledge, April 2026.*

### Personal Plan
The first thing a user sees after onboarding. The plan is generated based on which entry funnel the user came through. Current funnel tracks:

| Funnel | Target audience |
|---|---|
| **Learn AI to Earn Money** | Freelancers wanting to monetize AI skills |
| **Make Your AI** | Artists who want to produce AI music and earn from it |
| **Make Your AI Influencer** | People wanting to build & monetize an AI persona |
| **Learn Cloud** | Those wanting cloud/technical AI skills |

Each funnel generates a tailored learning roadmap shown as the user's Personal Plan.

---

### AI Chat
Built-in multi-AI chat hub inside the app. Users can converse with different AI models without leaving Jobescape.

- **Gated by tokens** — each user has a token balance that gets consumed per message
- **Token top-up is an upsell** — running out prompts a purchase flow
- Provides bundled access to models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) through Jobescape's API wrapper

---

### Assistants (Mini-Tools)
Focused single-task AI tools for specific job-related tasks. Current known assistants:

- **CV Transformer** — rewrites/optimizes resumes
- **Employment Assistant** — job application support
- **Personal Bio Generator** — writes professional bios
- More mini-tools for small, repeatable professional tasks

---

### Automation
Pre-built n8n automation templates that users can immediately deploy.

- Ready-to-use automations — no setup from scratch required
- Tied to the n8n learning track (teach → use → sell)
- One of the core skill tracks that leads to freelance upsell (users learn automations they can resell to clients)

---

### Prompt Library
Centralized library of curated prompts organized by use case. Users can browse and use prompts relevant to their track.

---

### Portfolio Builder + Hosting
As part of the course, Jobescape generates a freelancer portfolio website for the user. Once built, publishing it triggers a paid upsell.

**What gets built:** A professional portfolio site (example: "Aleba Studio — AI Automation Specialist") with skill tags (LLM Application, Automation, AI Enablement, etc.) and a client-facing CTA.

**Upsell mechanics ("Publish + get all templates"):**
| Item | Price |
|---|---|
| Template bundle (one-time) | $4.99 |
| Portfolio hosting (monthly, recurring) | ~~$10.99~~ $0.99/month |
| **Total today** | **$5.98** |

**What's included:**
- Hosted at `portfolio.jobescape.me/[username]` — live 24/7
- 18 templates, switchable any time
- Bonus: new features & templates added for free

**Why this works as an upsell:** The portfolio is created as a course deliverable — users feel it's "theirs" before the paywall appears. Publishing is the natural completion step, making the $5.98 feel trivial for something already built. The $0.99/month recurring component is the real LTV driver.

---

### Job Hunter
A dedicated job search and application optimization tool (appears to be a sub-product or separate site within the Jobescape ecosystem).

**Features:**
- **Job discovery** — finds vacancies matching the user's profile
- **Resume personalization** — tailors the user's existing resume to a specific job posting
- **Resume scoring** — grades the resume for a specific position: what's working, what's missing
- **Overall resume audit** — general quality assessment independent of a specific role
- **AI Interview Prep** — practice and coaching for interviews
- **Application tracker (Job Board)** — tracks all submitted applications in one place

**Value prop:** All-in-one platform that makes job applications fast, personalized, and data-driven.

## Upsell & LTV Strategy

Jobescape's monetization is built around **contextual upsells triggered by the user's chosen learning path**. The goal is to maximize LTV by serving relevant products at the right moment in the user journey.

### Upsell Map by Funnel

| User track | Core upsell | What they get |
|---|---|---|
| AI Influencer | Genescape credits | Build their AI avatar; generate content to grow as an influencer |
| AI Musician | Genescape credits | Generate music tracks and artist identity assets |
| Job seeker | Job Hunter (premium features) | Personalized resumes, interview prep, application tracking |
| Automation / n8n | Pre-built automation presets | Ready-made n8n workflows they can sell to clients as a freelancer |

### Upsell Layers (stacked)
1. **Token top-ups** — AI Chat tokens run out; users buy more
2. **Portfolio publish** — portfolio is built for free during the course; publishing it costs $5.98 upfront + $0.99/month hosting (see below)
3. **Genescape credits** — generation limits drive top-ups at $0.01/credit
4. **Course upgrades** — premium tracks, add-on toolkits ($29.99)
5. **Prompt Library** — paid prompt packs ($15.99–$39.99)
6. **Platform cross-sell** — Jobescape → Genescape subscription or credit bundle

### Why it works
The funnel teaches a skill → the user wants to practice it → the tool to practice it is the upsell. Learning and doing are collapsed into one product loop, which increases both conversion and perceived value.
