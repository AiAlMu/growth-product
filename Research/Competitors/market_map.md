# Competitive Market Map — JobEscape

*Last updated: April 2026*

---

## How to read this map

Three categories, defined by two axes:
- **Audience overlap** — do they target the same person (non-technical, wants to earn with AI)?
- **Format overlap** — do they use the same delivery model (mobile-first subscription + tools)?

---

## Category 1 — Direct Competitors

Same audience. Same promise. Same format. These are the apps a user would choose *instead of* JobEscape.

| Company | URL | Core promise | Model | Mobile | Monthly traffic |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Coursiv** | coursiv.io | Master AI tools, earn with AI, 15 min/day | Mobile-first subscription | ✅ Strong (4.7 iOS, 4.0 Android) | 6.23M |
| **Tixu** | tixu.ai | Learn AI + 15 AI tools included, earn $2K/mo | Web-first subscription | ⚠️ Weak (10 ratings) | 583K |
| **Finestro** | finestro.io | AI skills for income, automation-focused | Web + app subscription | ⚠️ Weak (2.5 iOS) | Not found |

**Why they're direct:** Same non-technical audience, same "earn money with AI" positioning, same subscription model, same onboarding quiz funnel, same guarantee mechanics.

**Key difference from JobEscape:** Coursiv and Tixu are purely course + tools bundles. JobEscape adds Genescape (creative AI platform), Job Hunter, and portfolio hosting — more vertically integrated.

## Category 2 — Adjacent Competitors

Overlapping audience or overlapping format, but not both. Users might use these *alongside* JobEscape or choose one over the other depending on their specific goal.

### 2a — Same audience, different format

| Company | URL | Core promise | Format | Why adjacent |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Summary: Who JobEscape actually competes with

```
PRIMARY THREAT:    Coursiv (6.23M visits, $2M+/mo App Store, same model, 10x the scale)
SECONDARY THREAT:  Tixu (same model, better international spread, declining traffic)
WATCH:             Masterly (small but high-intent, same skills curriculum, growing)
IGNORE:            Zeely, Udemy, Make Academy (different game entirely)
```

## What this means for the analysis

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Who is JobEscape's real benchmark? | Coursiv — same model, proven at scale |
| Where is the clearest gap? | Coursiv has no Genescape, no Job Hunter, no portfolio tool — JobEscape is more vertically integrated |
| Where is JobEscape weakest vs. direct competitors? | Scale (~6x less traffic than Coursiv), organic search (9% vs. opportunity), international revenue spread |
| Who could surprise? | Masterly — small, high-engagement, US-focused, same curriculum, no mobile app yet |
